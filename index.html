Carbon Reduction Helper (proof-of-concept)
A one-stop-shop tool helping busy people reduce their carbon footprint quickly and easily.

Unlike other carbon footprint calculators, CRH maximises the chance of success by making it as easy as possible to make the most effective carbon footprint reduction choices.

It does this by
  • no more entering lots of data - just choose from a list of pre-calculated actions
  • get information about new technology
  • complete chosen actions with help of task template and note taking system
